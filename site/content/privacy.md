## Privacy Policy

**ZX Cloud Security** uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site. This helps us improve content and understand which advisories are most useful to our audience.

### What we collect
- Pages visited and time spent
- General location (country/region level only)
- Browser and device type
- How you arrived at the site

### What we do not collect
- Personal identifying information
- Email addresses via analytics (subscription is handled separately and securely)
- Any data is sold or shared with third parties

### Your choices
You can decline analytics tracking using the cookie notice at the bottom of any page. You can also install a browser extension such as the [Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Contact
For any privacy questions email **advisories@zxcloudsecurity.co.uk**
