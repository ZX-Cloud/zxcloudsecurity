INTERPOL's Operation Ramz has dismantled Sniper Dz, a long-running phishing-as-a-service platform that enabled criminals to launch phishing campaigns with minimal technical skill. The operation, involving 13 MENA countries, resulted in 201 arrests including the platform's primary administrator. The takedown removes a significant commodity threat infrastructure that lowered the barrier to entry for phishing attacks globally.


> **Security Architect's Take:** Use this takedown as a prompt to audit your organisation's anti-phishing controls — review email gateway rules, MFA enforcement, and credential monitoring to ensure residual Sniper Dz-generated phishing infrastructure (domains, kits, harvested credentials) no longer poses a risk to your environment.
