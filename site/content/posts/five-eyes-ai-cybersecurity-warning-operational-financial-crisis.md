The Five Eyes intelligence alliance has issued a joint advisory warning that AI is significantly amplifying the speed and scale of cyber attacks, meaning what were once manageable security incidents can rapidly escalate into major operational and financial crises. The guidance calls on senior business leaders — not just security teams — to take direct ownership of cybersecurity posture. This reflects a growing consensus that AI-enabled threats demand board-level accountability and updated incident response strategies.


> **Security Architect's Take:** Review your incident response and escalation playbooks with AI-accelerated attack timelines in mind — assume adversaries can move from initial access to widespread impact in hours, not days. Use this advisory as leverage to drive board-level engagement on cloud security investment and risk ownership.
