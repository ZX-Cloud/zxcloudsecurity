Anthropic released its Fable AI model in June 2026, only for the US government to classify it as a dangerous munition three days later and restrict foreign access via export controls. Unable to verify user nationality, Anthropic shut down access entirely. The incident highlights the growing tension between AI capability development and state-level security regulation.


> **Security Architect's Take:** Review your organisation's dependency on third-party AI APIs and model providers — this incident demonstrates that access can be revoked with minimal notice due to regulatory action outside the vendor's control. Consider building contingency plans or multi-provider strategies for any AI services integrated into critical workflows.
