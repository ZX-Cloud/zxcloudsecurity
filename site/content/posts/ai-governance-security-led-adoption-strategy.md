A growing number of employees are adopting AI tools at work, with or without formal organisational approval, creating shadow AI risks that security teams must address. Security leaders who proactively build governed, visible AI adoption pathways are better positioned to manage risk whilst enabling business value. Effective AI governance gives CISOs strategic influence by aligning security controls with operational needs rather than simply blocking usage.


> **Security Architect's Take:** Develop a formal AI usage policy and approved tooling catalogue now — if you don't provide a sanctioned path, employees will find their own. Integrate AI governance into your existing cloud security posture management to gain visibility over which AI services are being accessed and what data they are processing.
