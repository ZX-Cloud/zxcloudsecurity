Lumen Technologies scaled its asset inventory from 17,000 to 1.1 million assets by overhauling its exposure management programme, highlighting how dramatically underestimated asset counts create blind spots across security operations. A wider industry survey found that fewer than half of organisations consolidate asset and exposure data into a single view, meaning vulnerability and risk programmes are built on incomplete foundations. Accurate asset visibility is a prerequisite for effective exposure management at enterprise scale.


> **Security Architect's Take:** Audit your asset discovery tooling and cross-reference outputs across your CMDB, cloud provider APIs, and network scanners — significant discrepancies are common and will undermine any downstream vulnerability or risk programme. Consider adopting a unified asset intelligence platform to consolidate coverage across on-premises, cloud, and hybrid environments.
