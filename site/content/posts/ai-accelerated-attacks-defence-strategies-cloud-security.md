AI is enabling attackers to compress multi-day attack chains into minutes, using models to craft targeted phishing lures, select victims, and pivot across hosts faster than most security teams can respond. Traditional detection and response runbooks, designed for human-paced adversaries, are struggling to keep up with this shift in tempo. A webinar is being promoted to address how defenders can adapt their tooling and processes to match AI-driven attack speeds.


> **Security Architect's Take:** Review and stress-test your detection and automated response playbooks against AI-accelerated attack timelines — specifically, measure your mean time to contain against a minutes-long lateral movement window rather than hours, and consider where automated response actions (e.g. host isolation, credential revocation) can replace manual triage steps.
